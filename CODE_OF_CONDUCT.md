# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want LittleCoder to feel welcoming, practical, and respectful for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- be respectful
- be constructive
- focus on helping the project improve
- assume good intent

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment or personal attacks
- posting private information
- abusive or hostile communication
- spam or malicious behavior

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, or contributions that harm the health of the project community.
